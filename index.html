About Me

I'm Toren Snyder, a student at Brown University from the Bay Area studying Applied Math and Computer Science. At Brown, I'm a teaching assistant for the computer science department and a tutor through the Annenberg Institute for Education, where I implement research-backed techniques for helping high school students excel in math, computer science, and the SAT. Off campus, I work as a freelance software engineer for organizations in the Bay. In high school, I scored in the 99th percentile on both the SAT and ACT and extensively researched the admissions process for highly selective schools. I know firsthand the uncertainty and stress of elite college admissions, and I'm here to help you navigate it.
