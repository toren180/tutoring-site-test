About Me

I'm Toren Snyder, and as a Brown University student from the Bay Area, I know firsthand how overwhelming the modern college landscape has become. Perfect grades and test scores are often just the baseline, and getting into a top-tier university today requires a deliberate, well-built strategy. I built that exact strategy to secure my spot in the Ivy League, where I study Applied Math and Computer Science. I currently work as a teaching assistant for the computer science department and an SAT tutor through the Annenberg Institute. I help students connect the dots between their academics, activities, and essays into a single cohesive narrative: one that elite college admissions officers actually select for.
